Whilst building your brand there are essentially two dimensions of great importance, controllable outcomes and non-controllable outcomes. What I mean by this is that however great the quality of our work, it does not necessary directly influence the success of the final product and its acceptance with the audience. So we can qualify the work we do to aim for a film or game which is likely to be successful and we can control the quality of the work we produce. I would like to know besides luck and contacts how young and unknown composers can achieve escape velocity to bridge the monetisation gap?
