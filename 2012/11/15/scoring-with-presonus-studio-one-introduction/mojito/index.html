About the author

Nikola Jeremić

Contributing Editor

Born and raised in a small town of Sabac (Serbia), Nikola Jeremić is accomplished film and game composer and performer. His credits include the feature film "6 Days Dark", short films "Maiden's Well 2", "Milun(ka)", and short animated films "Wise Grandpa" and "Bagsalesman And The Monkeys". Nikola is a member of Audio Engineering Society(AES).
