With a physical presence in more than thirty countries across the globe, SCOREcast is expanding all the time with its regional meet-up community program. A SCOREcast Chapter Community<\/strong> consists of a regional group of SCOREcasters who meet regularly and endeavor together to build relationships and camaraderie outside the studio with other composers and media music professionals in their local area.<\/p>\n

What are\u00a0“SCOREcast Chapter Communities”?<\/h3>\n

SCOREcast Chapter Communities organize numerous meet-ups, seminars, outings and industry events throughout the year. Each chapter maintains its own unique schedule, which can be found inside each respective chapter’s Facebook group page (click on the banners below for access to each chapter group on Facebook).<\/p>\n

Chapter Community Admission Requirements<\/h3>\n

Each SCOREcast Chapter Community operates on Facebook as a “closed group”, meaning that you must be either invited in by a current member or you need to have at least two friends already enjoying membership within the group you are asking to join. Once you’ve requested admission into a chapter community, the administrators for your group will check your credentials. Please have the following materials freely accessible on the web so that our chapter admins can get you started in the group as quickly as possible:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. \n
    Potential members must be working professional composers in the film, television, theater, video game or in an alternative visual media industry.<\/h5>\n<\/li>\n
  2. \n
    Potential members must either a) Be associated via Facebook friendship with at least two current chapter members, or b) Be invited into the group by a current member.<\/h5>\n

    SCOREcast: UK’s Quarterly Meetup<\/p><\/div><\/li>\n

  3. \n
    Potential members must be appropriately located geographically in relation to the group they are applying for (e.g. UK residents are not admitted to the Hollywood group, or vice versa, etc.).<\/h5>\n<\/li>\n
  4. \n
    Potential members must display their REAL photo as their Facebook avatar. SCOREcast is a ‘community’ organization. The concept of genuine community cannot be built when someone is masquerading as Steve Jobs or Batman… or both.<\/h5>\n<\/li>\n
  5. \n
    Potential members must make visible their professional website address on their personal Facebook profile.<\/h5>\n<\/li>\n
  6. \n
    Potential members who are university students or are studying music or composition at the collegiate level must make visible their educational institution and year of degree or graduation on their personal Facebook profile.<\/h5>\n<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    *NOTE: All requirements must be met for admittance into a chapter community, no exceptions. We are very strict about these admission guidelines in order that the chapters not be overrun by people who are in fact not composers and to also filter out any automatic “bot” knock-knocks that may occur within Facebook’s infrastructure. Please respect our administrators and be patient \u2014 SCOREcast Chapter Communities are extremely popular and receive upwards of 30 to 50 requests per day. It may take a day or so, but if you have met all of the requirements, we’ll get you in. If you do NOT receive entry within 48 hours, please double-check the requirements to make sure you haven’t missed anything. If you’ve missed something, your request will be denied and you will not be notified of the denial.<\/p>\n
