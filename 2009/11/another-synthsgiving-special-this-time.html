Houston Haynes: A “Synthsgiving” special from Native Instruments

by SCOREcast Team · No Comments · in Uncategorized

Hey gang – I just received an email from Native Instruments on a “Synthsgiving” special they’re running – Absynth, FM8, Massive and Reaktor for 99 $/€ each until November 30th. I already have all of these through my Komplete 6 license, but I’m glad to see that they’re parting them out for those that only want to add one or two of these monsters to their kit.

I have been really pleased with Reaktor and Absynth – the two main reasons that I went for the full Komplete package. However, it’s FM8 that has really surprised me. It’s *so* much more than an FM synth. Aside from having the ability to create some really twisted sound textures, it’s also very CPU-friendly. I’m using it extensively on a project and it continues to amaze with its sound quality and versatility. Anyway, not to sound like a shill for NI – but when something in my kit really “brings the lumber” I can’t keep quiet about it.

:-)

Happy “Synthsgiving” everyone!
