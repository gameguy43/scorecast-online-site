WELCOME TO SCOREcast

SCOREcast is the internet's leading podcast experience for the film music professional. Founded in 2006, and co-hosted by Deane Ogden and Lee Sanders, SCOREcast now reaches over 11,000 listeners worldwide from iTunes and other podcast aggregates to serve as a resource for anyone currently working in or pursuing work in the film music industry.

SCOREcastOnline.com is the companion site to the SCOREcast podcast, where you'll find a hub of information and how-to's for film composers, orchestrators, music editors, copyists, players, contractors, engineers, and assistants, and a place to interact, share, and learn from people at all levels in the film music community.

Calling All Emerging Composers!

Here is an opportunity for anyone looking to get started and maybe needing a little scratch to do it.

The fifth annual Robert Helps Composition Competition and Festival will take place in February 2010, celebrating not only the life and legacy of the late pianist-composer, but also the contributions of leading composers, historians, theorists and musical thinkers on the American musical scene.
