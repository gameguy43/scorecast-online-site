Jeremy Mayne Wins SCOREcast’s 2nd Annual Cue Contest

Posted by SCO Staff on Monday, December 26, 2011 · 2 Comments 

Last week, SCOREcastOnline.com announced our 2nd Annual Cue Contest and the grand prize—Spitfire Audio’s SOLO STRINGS library. 548 entries later by an impressive showing of composers from all over the globe, a victor has emerged:

The Winner of SCOREcast’s 2nd Annual Cue Contest

and the new owner of Spitfire Audio’s SOLO STRINGS is

Jeremy Mayne

for his composition

“Contemplation”

(click here to listen)

 

About Jeremy Mayne’s “Contemplation”

Jeremy Mayne is a 28-year-old composer based in the United Kingdom. His winning cue, selected from 548 submitted entries, was written for piano, harp and solo cello. The cue was chosen by our adjudication panel of five A-list film and television composers for its outstanding marks in the following three areas:

  • Featured use of solo instrument
  • Production value
  • Utilization of limitations (instrument choices and compositional decisions)

Jeremy used the following plugins/sound libraries as his palette in “Contemplation”:

  • LASS First Chair Solo Cello
  • Native Instruments “Alicia’s Keys” Grand Piano
  • VSL Harp – the version included in Kontakt 5

Judges’ Comments

“I’d put the playing up against anything I’ve heard or recorded on my own stuff, whether here [Los Angeles] or in London.”

“Top notch writing!”

“The interplay between the piano and the harp is pretty advanced.”

“The ebb and flow of the piece is very organic. Great use of liquidity and motion.”

“Not only is he a gifted composer, but his skills as a player are on display here as well.”

Head over to Jeremy Mayne’s Soundcloud profile and give him some love of your own on his winning track!

Congratulations to Jeremy for winning the contest, and a very special thanks to all who submitted and entered the contest. The level of writing, crafting, and production in the SCOREcast composing community is simply some of the best out there!

Stay tuned for our next contest and your chance to win again… coming up VERY soon!
